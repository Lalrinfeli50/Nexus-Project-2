OUR STORY

Established in 2015, Nexus Info is a fine dining restaurant specializing in authentic Indian cuisine. Our journey began with a passion for flavors and a desire to provide an unforgettable dining experience. Over the years, we have become a landmark for food enthusiasts, offering a unique blend of traditional and contemporary dishes.

Our decor, inspired by India's rich cultural heritage, combined with modern aesthetics, creates a warm and inviting atmosphere. From our signature dishes to our eclectic decor, every element of Nexus Info is designed to offer a memorable culinary journey.
